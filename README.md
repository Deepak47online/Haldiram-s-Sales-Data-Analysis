# Haldiram-s-Sales-Data-Analysis
This project focuses on analyzing the sales performance of Haldiram's for the calendar years 2023 and 2024 using Power BI. The objective was to derive meaningful business insights and identify trends that can support data-driven decision-making.

The dataset consisted of transactional sales data, including revenue, units sold, product categories, regions, and time-based information. Data cleaning and transformation were performed using Power Query to handle missing values, standardize formats, and prepare the dataset for analysis.

Key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total revenue, unit sales, and average unit price were created using DAX measures. A comparative analysis between CY 2023 and CY 2024 was conducted to evaluate year-over-year growth, seasonal patterns, and category-wise performance.

Interactive dashboards were developed to visualize trends, including:

Monthly and quarterly sales trends
Top-performing products and categories
Regional sales distribution
Year-over-year growth comparison

The analysis revealed significant patterns in customer demand, highlighted high-performing product segments, and identified areas with growth potential. These insights can help in optimizing inventory, improving sales strategies, and enhancing overall business performance.
